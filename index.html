In a version of the future, our world faces a void seemingly impossible to fill. The glory, the joy, the love - fading memories of a not so distant past, haunting those who struggle to hold on to them.

Its weight falls heaviest on the shoulders of one: the fire stealer, the lightning king. Our champion embarks on a new journey to an uncertain destination, with only one thing painfully clear: The Game Is Dead.
